Our Story

Safety begins with good maintainance

Our primary focus on weekdays is to improve safety and maintenance on the DUT campus using our web-based application. This platform will allow students, teachers, and staff to submit campus maintenance needs, resulting in a more effective and organised approach to handling safety and maintenance issues. Recognising the critical importance of a safe and functioning learning environment, our solution focuses on overcoming the issues associated with disorganised maintenance and security operations on university campuses and residences. By centralising issue reporting, pass requests, and resource access, we want to streamline operations and improve overall efficiency.
